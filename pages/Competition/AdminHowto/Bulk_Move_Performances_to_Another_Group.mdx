1. From the Home page, click **Competitions**  
2. Select the desired competition, and click **View**  
3. From the **Performances** tab, locate the **Performance Group** and click **View**


**![mceclip0.png](../../../resources/article-images/13938420638355-Bulk-Move-Performances-to-Another-Group_1.png)****Note:** if the Competition has Performance Groups disabled, you will be routed directly to the performances' list.


4. Click the **checkbox** next to each performance to select some or all performances


![mceclip0.png](../../../resources/article-images/13938420638355-Bulk-Move-Performances-to-Another-Group_2.png)  
5. Once selected the **Bulk Action** button ![mceclip2.png](../../../resources/article-images/13938420638355-Bulk-Move-Performances-to-Another-Group_3.png)will be enabled, click it and select **Move to Another Group**


![mceclip1.png](../../../resources/article-images/13938420638355-Bulk-Move-Performances-to-Another-Group_4.png)


6. Select the desired group from the drop-down menu, and click **Save**. 


![mceclip2.png](../../../resources/article-images/13938420638355-Bulk-Move-Performances-to-Another-Group_5.png)
