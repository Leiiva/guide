<ClickableTitle>Slayte Education Credit Terms Defined</ClickableTitle>







**Exam Board A** wants to allow users to claim credits for examinations they have recently completed.


**Organization B** wants to confirm their employee’s certification for HR training modules.


**University C** wants to confirm student attendance at compulsory research seminars.


**Claim Program** allows users to claim for credits they have completed and contain all the settings, forms, templates, and claimable items for a Claim.


**Claimable Items** are the credits that you will set up, to allow users to claim within the Claimable Program.


**Claim Providers** are the official certification bodies that provide the courses/credits that Claimers can complete e.g. an examination board.


**Feedback Template** is will be the form that Claimers fill in to complete a Claim.


**Speaker Template** allows you to collect feedback on any speakers/instructors that may have been involved with a Claim Program.


**Verification Template** can be used as a way to confirm whether a user has completed the task they are claiming credits for.


**Ratings** allow you to collect feedback on variables linked to a Claim Program e.g. Venue Location for a seminar.


**Claimer** end-user that claims certificates.
