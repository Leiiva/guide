You can access real-time checked-in and registration status information for the speakers in the event. 


1. From the desired event, click **Speakers**


2. The Checked-in and Registration status is separated by columns in the speaker's list 


![mceclip0.png](../../../resources/article-images/12910593717523-Speaker-s-Check-in-Registration-Status_1.png)


From here, you can filter for only Registered/ Checked in or not Registered/ Checked in by clicking the filter button ![mceclip1.png](../../../resources/article-images/12910593717523-Speaker-s-Check-in-Registration-Status_2.png) next to each column. Additionally, you can overview the following information: 


* Session Count
* Invited On
* Last emailed
