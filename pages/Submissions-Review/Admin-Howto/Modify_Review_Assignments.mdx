Sometimes it is required to un/re-assign a review, e.g. when a reviewer wants to recuse themselves, or they are unavailable and a replacement needs/ has been found.


1. Navigate to the desired submission detail page 


2. Scroll to the **Reviews** section in the submission


3. Select **UNASSIGN** for the reviewers you would like to remove


4. **Search** and **Select** the user(s) you would like to add as reviewers
