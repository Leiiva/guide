![mceclip1.png](../../../resources/article-images/14290513417363-Review-an-Application_5.png)

![mceclip6.png](../../../resources/article-images/14290513417363-Review-an-Application_6.png)

![mceclip7.png](../../../resources/article-images/14290513417363-Review-an-Application_7.png)
