When you no longer would like to attend or be part of a previously enrolled session, you can easily remove it from your schedule to free this time for another session, if desired.


1. From the Home page, go to **Events** and click the desired event tile  
2. Click **My Schedule** from the left panel


![mceclip0.png](../../../resources/article-images/14425468455187-Remove-Sessions-From-My-Schedule_1.png)   
3. You can search the session by name or filter, and once located click **View**


![mceclip1.png](../../../resources/article-images/14425468455187-Remove-Sessions-From-My-Schedule_2.png)   
4. On the right top corner of the session title bar, click ![mceclip2.png](../../../resources/article-images/14425468455187-Remove-Sessions-From-My-Schedule_3.png)**Remove from My schedule**


**![mceclip4.png](../../../resources/article-images/14425468455187-Remove-Sessions-From-My-Schedule_4.png)**


5. Click **Yes** to continue


![mceclip5.png](../../../resources/article-images/14425468455187-Remove-Sessions-From-My-Schedule_5.png)


6. You will receive the following confirmation message


![mceclip6.png](../../../resources/article-images/14425468455187-Remove-Sessions-From-My-Schedule_6.png)
