## **Delete a Vote**


If you change your mind or would like to vote at another time, you can delete/remove your vote. 


1. From the **Call** main page, go to the Vote section and click **Vote**  
2. Click the submission tile holding your vote   
3. Click **Vote**


![mceclip11.png](../../../resources/article-images/13793353676435-Vote-for-a-Submission_11.png)   
4. From **Your Votes** window, click the **X** icon to the left of your vote


![mceclip12.png](../../../resources/article-images/13793353676435-Vote-for-a-Submission_12.png)  
5. Click **Yes** to continue


![mceclip13.png](../../../resources/article-images/13793353676435-Vote-for-a-Submission_13.png)  
6. You will receive the following confirmation message 


![mceclip14.png](../../../resources/article-images/13793353676435-Vote-for-a-Submission_14.png)
