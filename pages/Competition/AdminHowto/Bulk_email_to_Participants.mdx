1. From the Home page, click **Competitions**   
2. Select the desired competition, and click **View**   
3. From the **Performances** tab, locate the **Performance Group** to communicate to and click **View**


**![mceclip0.png](../../../resources/article-images/13936601897875-Bulk-email-to-Participants_1.png)****Note:** if the Competition has Performance Groups disabled, you will be routed directly to the performances' list.


4. Click the **checkbox** to select some or all Participants 


![mceclip1.png](../../../resources/article-images/13936601897875-Bulk-email-to-Participants_2.png)


5. Once selected the **Bulk Action** button  ![mceclip2.png](../../../resources/article-images/13936601897875-Bulk-email-to-Participants_3.png)will be enabled, click it and select **Email** from the list 


![mceclip1.png](../../../resources/article-images/13936601897875-Bulk-email-to-Participants_4.png)


6. From the Send E-mail window, first select the **Recipient(s)** to be **Applicants only** or **All Participants**  
7. Type a **Subject** and the **Body** of the message. Type @ to use a placeholder, and click or press TAB to confirm


![mceclip3.png](../../../resources/article-images/13936601897875-Bulk-email-to-Participants_5.png)  
8. Optional. By default, **Send me a Copy** is selected, you can remove it if you don't want to receive a copy of the message  
9. Click **Send**


**![mceclip4.png](../../../resources/article-images/13936601897875-Bulk-email-to-Participants_6.png)**


10. You will receive the following confirmation message


![mceclip5.png](../../../resources/article-images/13936601897875-Bulk-email-to-Participants_7.png)
