1. Go to **Competitions**, click **View** for the desired Competition  
2. On the **Performances** Tab, locate the Group holding the performance and click **View**. In case of Performances Group is disabled, you will be displayed the list of performances instead.  
3. From the performance list, click **View** to the performance to update


![mceclip0.png](../../../resources/article-images/13985304466963-Withdraw-a-Performance_1.png)


4. In the **Application** **section**, click the **Edit** button


![mceclip1.png](../../../resources/article-images/13985304466963-Withdraw-a-Performance_2.png)


5. Click **Discard** at the bottom to be routed to the Performance details page


![mceclip1.png](../../../resources/article-images/13985304466963-Withdraw-a-Performance_3.png)


6. Click **Withdraw Application** 


![mceclip2.png](../../../resources/article-images/13985304466963-Withdraw-a-Performance_4.png)


7. You will receive the following confirmation message


![mceclip4.png](../../../resources/article-images/13985304466963-Withdraw-a-Performance_5.png)


8. Click Back to Competitions from the left top corner to return to the performances list page. 


![mceclip5.png](../../../resources/article-images/13985304466963-Withdraw-a-Performance_6.png)
