<ClickableTitle>How to Edit an Application Review</ClickableTitle>




1. From the Home page, click **Competitions** from the left panel or the **Competition tile**


![mceclip0.png](../../../resources/article-images/14289850205331-How-to-Edit-an-Application-Review_1.png)


2. Click the **Category** **tile** the desired competition is under


![mceclip1.png](../../../resources/article-images/14289850205331-How-to-Edit-an-Application-Review_2.png)


3. Click the competition tile holding the review 


![mceclip2.png](../../../resources/article-images/14289850205331-How-to-Edit-an-Application-Review_3.png)


4. In the **Review Performances** section, click View to the right of the Review you would like to edit 


![mceclip9.png](../../../resources/article-images/14289850205331-How-to-Edit-an-Application-Review_4.png)


5. Click **Edit**


![mceclip10.png](../../../resources/article-images/14289850205331-How-to-Edit-an-Application-Review_5.png)


6. Once you have completed the necessary changes, click **Preview**


![mceclip6.png](../../../resources/article-images/14289850205331-How-to-Edit-an-Application-Review_6.png)


7. A Preview of the review will be displayed, click **Submit** to continue


![mceclip7.png](../../../resources/article-images/14289850205331-How-to-Edit-an-Application-Review_7.png)


8. You will receive the following confirmation message 


![mceclip11.png](../../../resources/article-images/14289850205331-How-to-Edit-an-Application-Review_8.png)
